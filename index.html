01 — About
"I am not looking for an easy path. I am looking for the right one."

I am Muhammad Junaid — a student, aspiring researcher, published science author, Urdu poet, and servant of community from Topi, Swabi, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan.

My academic life has never fit neatly into a single box. While completing my higher secondary science education, I simultaneously earned an Islamic Studies Diploma with Jayyid Jiddan (Very Good) from Wifaq ul Madaris Al-Arabia. I publish science articles in Urdu, write poetry in the tradition of Allama Iqbal, lead community chapters, volunteer with Alkhidmat Foundation, and compete in talent competitions across KPK — all while pursuing international scholarships on seven continents.

My personal library holds over 405 books. I have been nominated for the prestigious Stipendium Hungaricum scholarship. I have appeared on television. I hold first-place prizes in multiple district and tehsil-level competitions. This is a life devoted entirely to knowledge and purpose.
